About

I am a multi-disciplinary artist and designer based in the north west of the UK. My work as an artist implements the context of electronic music culture & sound with the urban environment and subculture creating a combination of abstract and figurative artworks through mediums of photography, painting, printmaking and Collage.


The ambitions of my artistic practice is to bring art & music to the public space through exhibitions, public murals and live performances apllying the artforms of typography, abstract lettering and audio-visual installations that connect my creative influences. The purpose and aim of my creative output is to provide insight into dance music culture and it’s sound building an accelerated positive change on the public space.


Along with my own personal projects, I am open to comission based work from clients as well as public & community projects outdoor or indoor. If you have any requests or questions, please get in touch using the contact section of my website and/or through my social media channels.

Portfolio

My portfolio is a collection of works made during my personal projects which range from Digital Art, Paintings, Mix-media, Photography and Prints made in a contemporary art context. The work ranges from figurative to abstract work highlighting a diverse ability to work with various materials within similar contextual influences.
